    • ♦ Quick-Scan

  • Click on "Quick-scan" button
  • Scan with Qr Code
  • After scanning the Qr code, the app might display information such as contact details, website links, or other actions. Review the information and use it as needed.
  • Depending on the information contained within the QR code, you might have options to save contact details to your phone's address book, open a website, or perform other actions. Follow the prompts provided by the app to complete these actions.

  • Scan with OCR
  • Using your chosen OCR app, follow the instructions to capture an image of the business visiting card. Make sure the lighting is adequate and that the card is well-centered.
  • After capturing the image, the OCR software will analyze the image and convert the text on the business card into editable digital text. The process might be automatic, or you might need to initiate the OCR function.
  • Once the OCR process is complete, the app will provide you with the recognized text. Review the extracted text to ensure accuracy, as OCR isn't always perfect and might make mistakes, especially with fancy fonts or distorted images.
  • After confirming the accuracy of the extracted text, you can save it as a digital file.

    • FAQ

    1). What Does Expo lead app Do?
    Ans. The Expo lead app scans user information, saving time by automating data entry and maintaining digital records for enhanced follow-up.

    2). How can I add a contact?
    Ans. When you click on the Quick scan card to scan the QR code, you will get the user's details.

    3). How do I save to the ExpoList?
    Ans. You will get the user's details after clicking on the 'Save to ExpoList' button to add their information to the ExpoList

    4). How can I save to the phonebook?
    Ans. You can save user details by clicking on the 'Save to Phonebook' option.

    5). How can I view details about the user?
    Ans. When you click on the 'Action' option from list, it will display all the details of a particular user

    6). How can I edit user details?
    Ans. There are two ways to edit user details:
    1. From the Expo List: Click on the 'Action' button on the right side, then select 'Edit' to modify the details.
    2. From the user's details view: Click on the pencil icon located in the top right corner. After making the necessary edits, click on the 'Save' button to save the updated user details.

    7). How can I delete user details?
    Ans. There are two methods to delete a user's details:
    1. From the Expo List: Click on the 'Action' button on the right side, then choose the 'Delete' option. A popup message will appear with two buttons: 'Yes' and 'No.' Selecting 'Yes' will delete the user's details, while choosing 'No' will retain them.
    2. From the user's details view: Click on the delete icon located in the top right corner. A popup message will appear with two options: 'Yes' and 'No.' Opting for 'Yes' will delete the user's details, whereas clicking 'No' will prevent their details from being deleted.

    8). How do I add notes?
    Ans. There are two methods to add notes:
    1. From the user details view: Click on the plus icon to add a note. Write your note and then click the 'Add' button. The added note will be visible in the notes section.
    2. From the Expo List: In the 'Action' options, select 'Add Notes.' Then, by clicking 'View,' you can see and manage the added notes.

    9). How can I edit notes?
    Ans. To edit notes, follow these steps:
    1. From the notes view, click on the edit icon located on the right side.
    2. A popup message will appear, allowing you to edit the note's content.
    3. Make the desired changes to the note.
    4. Click the 'Update' button to save the edited notes.

    10). How do I delete notes?
    Ans. To delete notes, follow these steps:
    1. From the notes view, locate the note you wish to delete and click on the delete icon on the right side.
    2. A popup message will appear with two options: 'Yes' and 'No.'
    3. Click 'Yes' to confirm and delete the notes, or click 'No' to retain them.

    11). How can I add a favorite?
    Ans. There are three ways to add to favorites:
    1. From the Expo List: Click on the star icon located on the right side to mark as a favorite.
    2. From the Expo List: Click on the 'Action' button on the right side, then select 'Add to Favorite' from the options.
    3. From the side menu: Choose the 'Favorites' option to add to favorites.

    12). How can I view my profile?
    Ans. There are two methods to view your profile:
    1. From the left side menu: Click on your image or email to access your profile.
    2. From the left side menu: Select the 'Profile' option to view your profile.

    13). How do I edit my profile?
    Ans. To edit your profile, follow these steps:
    1. From the left side menu, select 'Profile' to view your profile.
    2. On the profile screen, click on the pencil icon located at the top right corner.
    3. Edit your profile information as needed.
    4. Click the save icon at the bottom right corner to update your profile.

    14). How can I log out of the app?
    Ans. To log out of the app, follow these steps: From the left side menu, select 'Logout.'Click on the 'Logout' button to confirm and log out of the app.
